About AMARA Salon

Where beauty, elegance, and luxury meet.

Who We Are

AMARA is a modern beauty salon offering premium services crafted with care, precision, and passion. Our mission is to provide an experience that blends relaxation, luxury, and flawless results.

Founded as a Computer Science graduation project supervised by Dr. Balsam Khoja, AMARA reflects both technical excellence and artistic creativity.

What We Offer

Our salon provides a wide range of services including hair styling, makeup artistry, nail treatments, skincare sessions, and relaxation packages. Every service is performed with high standards and attention to detail, ensuring our clients leave feeling confident and refreshed.
